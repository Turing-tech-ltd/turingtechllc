About Us

welcome to turing tech

Turing Tech is a technology company specializing in tech skills training, consulting, outsourcing, software development, incubation and strategic partnerships. Originally founded in the USA as a tech stack training startup with a focus on Frontend Development, Turing Tech has rapidly evolved to meet the growing demands of the global tech ecosystem.

Today, our training portfolio includes a diverse range of in-demand disciplines; Frontend Development, Backend Development, Mobile Application Development (Android), IT Assurance & GRC, Data Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Blockchain Technology.

Driven by a mission to democratize tech education and innovation, Turing Tech has expanded its reach beyond the USA to Canada, the United Kingdom, Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, and Zimbabwe — equipping thousands with practical, job-ready tech skills and fostering partnerships across continents.

our vision

To raise a generation of tech engineers who come from various tech and non-tech disciplines, to teach them to build solutions that add value to society and to their nations, and hopefully, to transform them into techpreneurs capable of building the next unicorn
