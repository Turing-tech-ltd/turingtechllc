FAQ

What is the course fee?

Each course costs $ US 7,000 (Seven Thousand US Dollars).
This sum can be paid in full or in installments.

What background knowledge is necessary?

The courses are suitable for those with and without knowledge of computer science or without a technical background. However, a basic knowledge of computer literacy is needed. This knowledge could include anything from keyboarding skills to creating files and folders, copying and pasting of texts and files, working with files and folders and a general desire to learn online.

What are cohorts and how do they work?

Our programs are taught in cohorts, meaning you'll learn alongside a group of peers from start to finish. Each cohort fosters collaboration, accountability, and shared growth through live sessions, group projects, and discussions that simulate real-world tech teamwork. You'll benefit from peer support, direct access to mentors and instructors, and continuous feedback, all while building lasting professional connections. Cohort sizes vary by course, but we keep them small enough for personalized attention, so you're never learning alone.

What schedule options do you offer?

We provide full-time and part-time programs designed to fit your lifestyle, whether you're studying, working, or balancing both. Each schedule includes live classes, mentorship, projects, and structured milestones to help you stay consistent and on track.

What if I miss a class or need more flexibility?

All sessions are recorded and accessible anytime, so you'll never fall behind. You can also switch between full-time and part-time (within the first few weeks) if your availability changes. Regardless of your schedule, you'll receive the same content, mentorship, and career support.

What is lecture mode, and how does it enhance learning?

Lecture mode is a live, instructor-led learning experience where you actively engage with experts through real-time teaching, Q&A sessions, coding demos, polls, and group discussions. It's designed to simulate a real classroom, encouraging interaction, collaboration, and instant feedback. All sessions are also recorded, so you can revisit lessons anytime and learn at your own pace.

Do I need coding experience to be enrolled?

No!
Enrolees do not require any prior programming skills. Turing Tech will start each enrolee from beginner level and transform them into subject matter experts by the end of the program.

Is the Program Full Time?

Each course requires six (6) hours per week of live classes. These classes take place three (3) times a week. Each class lasts two (2) hours. Classes are scheduled after work. We make efforts to accommodate different time zones.

Will I get a job after this course?

Not only will we teach you up-to-date industry skills, we will also work you through an interview preparation process and make efforts to connect you to prospective jobs.
